Exploring Novels in Hispaniola

This map showcases important locations in the novels "The Farming of Bones" by Edwidge Danticat and "The Brief Wondrous Life of Oscar Wao" by Junot DÃ­az.

These two novels take place on the Island of Hispaniola, and at some points in the novels, during the same time period.

Many times, it is difficult to understand the connection between places in novels given the medium of writing.
The goal of this project is to map important locations in these novels to provide a connection to the real world and help build context and setting that reading alone cannot do.

The pins on the map represent locations. Clicking or tapping on a pin will open a box that gives more information about that location.
The menu on the right side lists the locations. Click or tap on a location to jump to it and open it automatically. Feel free to scroll around get a sense of where these pins exist in relation to each other.
